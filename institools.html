    INSTITOOLS

  • INSTITOOLS

    • Basic Info

    InstiTools is a H.I.T inaugural spin-off company of the department of Industrial and Manufacturing Engineering. The InstiTools workshop is a one- stop job shop environment that offers a full range of engineering requirements from machine equipment, plant installation, CAD drawing, mold production, design of customised products, systems analysis, instrumentation and control engineering. InstiTools offers quality, value adding products and services that boost output and productivity in the following sectors:

    Industrial equipment

    Egg incubators, Solar dryers, Bottle moulds, machined gears, nuts, threaded bolts, Face shields

    Services

    Non-destructive testing, CAD, pre-trade test preparation, tooling technology training, refrigeration & air conditioning service & repair.Insti tools of late have been majoring in the bulk making of face shields, solar dryers and moulds.
